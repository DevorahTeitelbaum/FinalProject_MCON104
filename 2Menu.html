Who We Are and What We Do

Book Nook

Here at the Book Nook, we take pride in not just selling books, but fostering a connection to reading. We give our readers an experience, not just the ability to purchase books. From caring literary experts to help you with the tough choice of which book to choose, to events that connect you, your friends, and family to reading, we truly open the door to experiencing the joy that a good book can give.

Our Curated Collection

Besides for all the classics and trending reads, we have a collection of antique books for sale. These are rare finds, old printings, and signed copies of famous books. Ask in store to be shown the collection.

Events

Several times throughout the week, we have events for both adults and kids. In-person book signings, meet-and-greets with your favorite authors, and special Mommy-and-Me storytime are just part of the fun. We also change our schedule throughout the year to keep things exciting. Keep an eye out for seasonal events, like our Spring outdoor reading day, or our Winter reading challenge checklist. This is in addition to our one of a kind annual event starting on World Book Day and continuing for the week.
